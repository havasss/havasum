Confligure Price Quote

The Configure Price Quote (CPQ) system is a vital tool for HKT’s internal use. It is responsible for a wide range of functions, including product selection, payment processing, appointment creation, goods returns, advance orders, and more. This project is built using the Salesforce system, which means that I have to devote time to studying Salesforce design components to ensure that the CPQ system runs smoothly.
As a member of the UI/UX team, my role is multi-faceted. I work closely with the Business Analyst team to review the new requirements of what users want before starting on any UI design. This allows us to create a product that is tailored to the specific needs of our users. Additionally, as the contact role of the UI/UX team for the CPQ project, I oversee its implementation. This means that I am responsible for ensuring that the project runs smoothly and that any issues are dealt with promptly.
