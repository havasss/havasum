UI Design

HKTDC

The Hong Kong Trade Development Council (HKTDC) is a statutory body established in 1966 to promote, assist, and develop Hong Kong’s trade.
HKTDC has an online sourcing website for connecting global buyers and suppliers. Now, they want to improve their sourcing website. As the UI team, we are working with the UX team to redesign the sourcing website and dashboard system based on their research.
As it is a large website that includes both buyer and seller platforms, I am sharing the workload with another UI designer under a very tight deadline.
