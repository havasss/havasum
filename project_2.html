SmartCharge

SmartCharge App is a mobile application created by HKT and CLP to address the entire process of charging electric vehicles (EVs). The app aims to provide a user-friendly and efficient charging experience for EV owners.
However, the original SmartCharge App was difficult to use and lacked a clear purpose, which resulted in low adoption rates. In response, the development team decided to restructure and enhance the UI/UX and the app’s functions to attract more users.
