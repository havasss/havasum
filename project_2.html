SmartCharge

SmartCharge App is a mobile application created by HKT and CLP to address the entire process of charging electric vehicles (EVs). The app aims to provide a user-friendly and efficient charging experience for EV owners.
However, the original SmartCharge App was difficult to use and lacked a clear purpose, which resulted in low adoption rates. In response, the development team decided to restructure and enhance the UI/UX and the app’s functions to attract more users.
This is just the very first stage of the project and the team is working to improve the app’s user experience and functionality. As the UI/UX lead for this project, I have come up with a draft of the look and feel to showcase to HKT based on the new features that they provided in a PPT.
The updated SmartCharge App seeks to provide users with a more intuitive and streamlined experience, with features such as real-time updates on charging status and nearby charging stations. These updates will make the app more user-friendly and efficient, making it easier for EV owners to locate and use charging stations.
With these improvements, the SmartCharge App aims to attract more users and become a go-to platform for EV charging.
